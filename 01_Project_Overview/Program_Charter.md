# Program Charter — Referral Doctor Digital Presence
## Yashoda Hospitals | Digital Marketing Initiative

---

## 1. Program Context

Yashoda Hospitals maintains a large network of referral doctors — general practitioners, specialists,
and consultants operating small-to-mid-sized clinics across Hyderabad and surrounding areas.
These doctors are:
- The primary patient pipeline for Yashoda's specialties
- Often digitally underrepresented (no website, weak GMB, no social presence)
- Operating in competitive local catchment areas where patients Google before visiting

**The Opportunity:** A structured digital presence for each referral doctor increases their
local discoverability → more walk-ins to their clinic → more referrals to Yashoda.
It also builds loyalty (doctors feel Yashoda is investing in their success).

---

## 2. Program Goals

| Goal | Metric | Target (12 months) |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Digital presence coverage | % of referral doctors with GBP set up | 80% |
| Content consistency | Monthly posts per doctor | ≥ 4 |
| Review volume | Average Google reviews per doctor | +20 in year 1 |
| Referral increase | Patient referral volume uplift | +15–25% |
| Doctor satisfaction | NPS from enrolled doctors | ≥ 70 |

---

## 3. Doctor Personas

### Persona A — The Clinic GP (Most Common)
- MBBS, practicing 5–15 years
- Runs a small general practice clinic
- Minimal digital presence (only a phone number on JustDial)
- Relies entirely on word-of-mouth
- Low digital literacy; time-poor
- **Needs:** Turnkey setup, WhatsApp-first communication, zero overhead

### Persona B — The Mid-Tier Specialist
- MD/MS in a specialty (Cardiology, Ortho, Gynecology, etc.)
- Associated with 1-2 hospitals
- Has a basic Facebook page but no active management
- Wants recognition, patient reviews, and professional content
- **Needs:** Specialty-specific content, co-branded posts, LinkedIn presence

### Persona C — The Aspiring Digital Doctor
- Younger doctor (< 10 years practice)
- Actively using Instagram, understands the value of digital
- Wants to build a personal brand alongside Yashoda association
- **Needs:** Reels content, personal branding strategy, patient education series

---

## 4. Program Scope

### In Scope
- Doctors in Yashoda's active referral network (Tier 1 & Tier 2)
- Hyderabad metro area (Phase 1), expanding to Tier-2 cities (Phase 2)
- Digital channels: Google, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Practo, JustDial

### Out of Scope (Phase 1)
- Paid ad campaign management (offered as add-on)
- EMR / clinic management software integration
- Teleconsultation platform setup

---

## 6. Program Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Month 1–3)
- Onboard 25 pilot doctors
- Set up GBP, social pages, Practo profiles
- Deliver starter content pack (10 posts per doctor)
- Deploy WhatsApp Business setup

### Phase 2 — Scale (Month 4–8)
- Expand to 100+ doctors
- Automate content publishing via AI pipeline
- Launch reputation management (review QR codes, templates)
- Monthly analytics reporting per doctor

### Phase 3 — Intelligence (Month 9–12)
- AI-personalized content per specialty
- ChatGPT/Gemini AI search optimization (GEO)
- Doctor-level dashboard with live insights
- Cross-referral analytics linking digital presence to Yashoda patient intake
