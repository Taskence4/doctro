## SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

### Why Referral Doctors Need This Now

**The Search Reality:**
- 77% of patients Google a doctor's name before their first visit
- 68% will not visit a doctor with fewer than 10 Google reviews
- "Best [specialty] near me" searches have grown 200%+ in 3 years
- AI-powered search now surfaces local doctor recommendations

**The Gap:**
Most referral doctors in India still have:
- No website or a broken one
- Incorrect or incomplete Google Business Profile
- 0-3 Google reviews
- Inactive or non-existent social media
- No consistent patient messaging
- No presence in AI search results

**The Cost of Invisibility:**
A doctor with weak digital presence can lose 3-5 potential new patients per week to better-positioned competitors.
