# Executive Pitch Summary
## Digital Presence Program for Referral Doctors
### AI Lab - One-Page Brief

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY IN ONE LINE
> Every day, patients Google a referral doctor before their appointment.
> Most small-clinic doctors are still invisible online. We fix that at scale using AI.

---

## THE PROBLEM

| Stat | Implication |
|------|-------------|
| 77% of patients search doctor names online | Invisible doctors lose trust before the first visit |
| Most referral doctors have <5 Google reviews | Patients choose competitors with stronger social proof |
| AI search (ChatGPT, Google AI) now recommends doctors | Unoptimized doctors get excluded from new discovery channels |
| Traditional agency retainers often cost Rs.20K-Rs.50K/month | Too expensive for many independent doctors and small clinics |

---

## OUR SOLUTION: AI-POWERED DIGITAL PRESENCE KIT

We deliver a turnkey, AI-powered digital presence system for each referral doctor:

**6 Core Pillars:**
1. **Local Discovery** - GBP, landing page, directory listings
2. **Content Engine** - AI-generated posts, Reels, WhatsApp cards
3. **Social Media** - Managed Instagram/Facebook with branded content
4. **Reputation** - Review QR codes, response management, Practo optimization
5. **Patient Communication** - WhatsApp flows for reminders, follow-ups, and tips
6. **AI Search (GEO)** - Visibility in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI results
