## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

A productized, done-for-you digital growth service for referral doctors.

The business logic:
> Better digital visibility → more patient trust → more appointments and repeat visits → measurable clinic growth → doctor loyalty to AI Lab

**Positioning:** Premium enough to signal quality. Accessible enough for a working specialist. AI-powered enough to justify every rupee.

**Sales target:** 80% of enrollments should be Tier 2 (Growth Engine). Tier 1 is the entry hook; Tier 3 is the high-margin aspirational offer.

*"Get Found. Get Reviews. Get on the Map."*
